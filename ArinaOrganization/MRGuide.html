品階說明頁
(Guide Page of Moral Ranking)

在有奈教中我們把人的品格分為以下的等級，階級之後為代表的身分

5階 巫女、君子
4階 品女、士
3階 侍女、執事、專業人員
2階 啦啦隊、一般工作人員、兵
1階 小朋友、新手
0階 未入教、不認識
-1階 騙子
-2階 罪犯
-3階 奴隸(是騙子同時也是罪犯)
-4階 死囚

教主算6階，實際上也是第5階

In Arinaism, we divide the moral level of people into the following ranks, after the rank number is the iconic status

Rank 5 Miko, Junzi
Rank 4 girl of virtue, Shi
Rank 3 maid, deacon, professional
Rank 2 cheerleader, worker/staff, soldier
Rank 1 child, novice
Rank 0 non-member, unknown person
Rank -1 liar
Rank -2 criminal
Rank -3 slave (a liar with crimes)
Rank -4 death row

Master is counted as rank 6, which is either rank 5.


1階以上，叫做好人
-1階~-3階，尚有贖罪可能的壞人，-3的話死掉也沒關係
-4階 必須死的壞人

Rank 1 or higher are good people.
Rank -1 to -3 are bad people who are guilty but have a chance to atone. It doesn’t matter if one died at rank -3.
Rank -4 means wicked people who must die.


如果是-1階的處罰對象，教外的人也不能處罰，只有加入有奈教的2階以上人員才能處罰-1階的人
-2階以下的人教外人士可以逕行處罰，不能對-2階的犯人造成身體的永久傷害及死亡
-3階的人只要沒有死亡，其生命和身體的所有權不會被教外人士奪去
-4階可任意處置

People who didn't join Arina Organization cannot punish rank -1, only rank 2 and higher rank people who join the Arina Organization can punish rank -1. One can punish people below rank -2 (include rank -2), but it is prohibited to cause permanent physical damage or death to the sinner of rank -2. As long as a rank -3 person is not dead, the ownership of his life and body will not belong to someone who didn't join the Arina Organization. Rank -4 can be disposed of at will.


要升為4階，必須要能獨立，並且完全不畏死亡，要升為5階，必須要有受辱、遭苦難也不改其志的意志力

To be promoted to rank 4, one must be independent and absolutely not fear death. To be promoted to rank 5, one must have a strong will to suffer humiliation and torments without changing one's mind.
