有奈教的價值觀(Arina's Values)

人的價值應該依以下四項標準來決定(按照順序)

People's value should be judged by 4 standards below(by order):

1.品格(Moralities)

2.技能(Skills)

3.外貌、禮(Appearance, Manners)

4.成就(Achievements)


有奈教的五項原則(美德)(Arina's 5 Principles of Virtue)

有奈教有五項原則(美德)要遵循

There are 5 principles of virtue in the Arina Organization that should be followed:

1.善良(Good)

2.誠實、正直(Honesty, Integrity)

3.和平(Peace)

4.優雅(Graceful)

5.勇敢(Valiant)
