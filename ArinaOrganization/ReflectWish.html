請願(Reflecting Wish)

請願是附帶捐款人的願望的特殊捐款。
因為需要表現誠信，需要親自和教主碰面。
表達願望，以及原因。
成願與否，有多少誠意及運勢將是重要因素，若是未能如願，有奈教不會返還捐款，同樣有捐款的功能。
長命之類的願望，建議捐款。


Reflecting Wish is a special donation that comes with the donor's wishes.
Because you need to show your integrity, you need to meet the Master in person.
Express your desire and reason.
Whether the wish comes true or not, sincerity and luck will be important factors. If the wish is not fulfilled, Arina Organization will not return the donation, it still has the function of Donate.
For wishes such as long live, Donate are recommended.


請願的最小金額是100萬有奈物資點。
The minimum amount of reflecting wish is 1 million Arina supply points.
