有奈教入教須知

Arina Organization Admission Agreement

有奈教有五項原則(美德)

身為有奈教的一員必須要至少遵守前三項

1.善良
2.誠實、正直
3.和平
4.優雅
5.勇敢

同時，每一位成員都應遵從教的指揮

關於和平，如果有教外的人攻擊，嘲笑或詆毀有奈、有奈教，教並不希望各位直接和對方發生衝突，你可以與他們辯論，但是不應爭論或是發生肢體衝突

在初期，我們並不認識各位，此時繳交的入教金即成為成員的信用保證，如有任何違反前三項原則或反對教領導的人出現時，將會因為他/她的行為被教剔除

只要一直維持原則，各位就能開始建立自己的信用，教也會統計這個相關項目，提升適任者擔任更高的職位

打擊邪惡，為本教的目標，有奈教會因為各位的成員資格為各位的良善人格提供保證，可加以利用，任何狡猾的行為都被嚴格禁止

雖然看起來路程遙遠並且難以達成，只要成員保持自身不受汙染，遵守原則，即可因為你對有奈的信仰進而擁有不會後悔的一生
There are 5 principles of virtue in the Arina Organization.

You must follow the first three to stay with us.

1.Good
2.Honesty, Integrity
3.Peace
4.Graceful
5.Valiantly

and you should follow Arina’s leadership.

About peace, sometimes non-good people will scoff or slander Arina, as a member of Arina Organization, you should be undisturbed and do not easily conflict with them, you may debate but not argue or fight with them.

At the start, we don’t know each other, as a promise, your admission fee will be your credit guarantee. If anyone violates the first three principles or goes against Arina’s leader, will be expelled from the organization for his/her bad conduct.

You will earn your credit by time, when times comes, Arina’s leader will promote you to the higher appropriate position in Arina Organization.

Defeat wickedness is our goal, Arina will give you alignment guarantee because your membership, cunning behavior is strictly prohibited.

Even if it will take a long run to reach our destination, as a member of Arina, keeping your moral integrity is up to standard and will bring no regret throughout your life since your Arina faith.
