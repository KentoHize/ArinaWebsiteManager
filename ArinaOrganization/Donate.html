捐款(Donate)

捐款至有奈教，實現正義。
人們生活在繁忙的現代社會中，無論多麼狡詐或是陰險，獲取或是未能獲取利益，結果總是身心俱疲。
唯有正義得勝時，人們才能夠避免邪惡/報復/災難。
即使是有罪之人，透過捐款，也能部分免除自身的罪。
除了捐款之外，有奈教也附有請願的功能，可以使人們的願望實現，詳見請願，普通來說，捐款能夠改變運勢。


Donate to the Arina Organization and achieve justice.
People live in a busy modern society, no matter how cunning or insidious you are, whether you gain or fail to gain benefits, the result is always physical and mental exhaustion.
Only when justice prevails, people can avoid evil, revenge, and disaster.
Even guilty people can partially absolve themselves of their sins by donating money.
In addition to donations, Arina Organization also has a reflecting wish function that can make people's wishes come true.
For details, see Reflecting Wish. Generally speaking, donations can change your fortune.

捐款目前只接受單次100萬有奈物資點以上的捐款。
Currently, only donations of 1 million Arina supply points or more are accepted.
