為美術應徵者(For Art Applicants)

我是有奈教教主，日瀬健人，下面是給予通過初次審查的人的挑戰

圖1是Leaf社的10,000,000次點擊慶祝圖，圖2是教主個人的接近圖。

1.證明自己的實力，試著畫出圖1(背景不用)，要上色。

2.畫出變形的圖(其他動作、其他服裝、類似特徵)。

如果有方法驗證，拿出足夠好的作品並標價，可以跳過這次測試，圖中角色並不是我們工作的目標。

將完成圖/樣品送至 KentoHize@arinaorganization.org

你應該在6天內能夠完成。


This is Master of the Arina Organization, 日瀬健人(Kento Hize), and here is a challenge for those who passed the initial review.

Sample 1 is a "Leaf's 10,000,000 clicks celebration" picture, and Sample 2 is a Master's personal approach picture.

1. To prove your ability, try to draw Sample 1 (without background) and color it.

2. Draw a variation. (other actions, other clothing, similar features).

3. Pricing. Perhaps this is the most important part. After all, there is no way to hire a painter whose price is too high.


For applicants outside Taiwan, we also need to know how to verify that the work is yours.

If there is a way to verify it, come up with a good enough work and put a price on it. You can skip this test.

The characters in the picture are not the target of our work.

Send finished drawing/sample to KentoHize@arinaorganization.org

You should complete this test within 6 days.
