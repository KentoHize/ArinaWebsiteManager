國家/跨國公司/跨國團體登記(Registration for Country/International Companies/International Group)

我們目前正接受國家、跨國公司以及跨國團體的登記作業。

採取一對一的模式，由有意願登記的國家/跨國公司/跨國團體以此信箱與有奈教進行接洽：

service@arinaorganization.org

之後登記團體應到訪有奈教報告與說明其國家/公司/團體的地理位置/持有領土/服務商圈/運作情況和其他必要內容。


歡迎。


We are currently accepting registrations from countries, international companies, and international groups.

Adopting one-on-one mode, countries/international companies/international groups willing to register can contact us via email:

service@arinaorganization.org

Later, the registration group should visit the Arina Organization to report and explain their location/territory/service area/operation status and other necessary information.


Welcome.

國家登記費用約為1億有奈物資點~10億有奈物資點(根據國家大小)。
The registration fee of countries is approximately 100 million to 1 billion Arina supply points (depending on the size of the country).

跨國公司登記費用約為2000萬有奈物資點~5億有奈物資點(根據公司大小)。
The registration fee of international companies is approximately 20 million to 500 million Arina supply points (depending on the size of the companies).

跨國團體登記費用另議。
The registration fee of international group will be discussed separately.
