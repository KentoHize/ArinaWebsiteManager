關於有奈教(About Arina Organization)

何謂有奈教？

有奈教是一個理想組織及理想世界，追尋真正的安全與自由，有奈教是哲學與教育機構。

有奈教監督人類的活動，防治惡行，也是人類最高監督組織。

有奈教在全世界提供教育、世事分析、國際糾紛仲裁、改善運勢、回應願望等等服務。

有奈教在有必要的時候可以被視為小國家，逮捕犯人。


What is the Arina Organization?

Arina Organization is an ideal organization and an ideal world that pursues true security and freedom.
Arina Organization is a philosophical and educational institution.

Arina Organization supervises human activities and prevents evil deeds.
It is also a supreme organization of supervision for humankind.

Arina Organization provides services such as education, international affairs analysis, international dispute arbitration, bestowing fortune, and refelecting wishes throughout the world.

Arina Organization can be treated as a small country and arrest criminals when necessary.
